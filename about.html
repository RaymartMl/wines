How We Started

When our company's founder, Chuck Norris has moved from his home city of Pasig to the Western Mandaluyong, he had just one dream: to continue his father's brewing business in Pasig...

He eventually succeeded in that, founding a brewery and a craft beer pub in a small town of Rodriguez...

The main idea that he laid down and which he tries to follow himself is to make as many craft beers/styles as possible, with as many different flavors to them as possible....
